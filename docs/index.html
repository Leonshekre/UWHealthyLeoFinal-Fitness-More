Cardio & Strength Training

The two most commonly referred to exercises are cardio and weightlifting. Cardio focuses on one's breathing and heart rate while weightlifting focuses on building muscle.

To stay healthy, one should try a mix of both. Doing this regularly throughout the week can drastically improve not just physical health, but mental health as well!


Strength Training/Weight lifting

This form of fitness is incredibly helpful to build more than just muscle. It helps with bone strength, weight loss, mental health, fat percentage, heart health, blood sugar levels, mobility, flexibility, and the list goes on.

Strength training seems intimidating at first, but it is much simpler than it seems. The greatest tip to begin weight lifting is...
